TURBINE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Stimutech proudly presents an APP , an advanced and user-friendly monitoring portal designed to elevate the wind turbine monitoring experience for our valued customers. Our commitment to excellence aligns with industry best practices, ensuring state-of-the-art technology for optimal wind turbine performance assessment.
Our App empowers Stimutech customers with secure user access control, enabling 24/7 real-time monitoring of their wind turbine portfolio. Whether on a PC, tablet, or smartphone, users can effortlessly track the performance of all their wind parks through the intuitive main dashboard. Navigating to the PARK section provides a comprehensive overview, displaying operational status, power, and wind speed for each turbine in the park. Additionally, users can access park-level reports detailing daily energy production and availability.
For a more granular view, selecting a specific turbine leads users to the TURBINE section, offering live operational data through a visually engaging interface. This section also provides turbine-level reporting, ensuring users have detailed insights into individual turbine performance. Alarms and warnings are seamlessly integrated into all sections, ensuring prompt notification of any issues.
To prioritize data security, each customer receives a personalized login registered on the Stimutech server. Our dedication to enhancing the customer experience is evident in our App, showcasing our ongoing investment in cutting-edge applications. Experience transparency and efficiency in turbine performance data with Stimutech's APP.
